# archive

This directory preserves **superseded documents** for historical context.

- **Nothing here is normative.** These files are kept only so future readers can see what an earlier
  design looked like and why it changed. They are not maintained and may contradict the current
  design.
- **For the current, authoritative design**, see [`../docs/adr/`](../docs/adr/) (the decision record)
  and [`../docs/architecture/`](../docs/architecture/) (the architecture docs). Where anything here
  disagrees with an ADR, the ADR wins.
- **What does _not_ belong here:** research inputs (they stay in
  [`../docs/research/`](../docs/research/) with a point-in-time banner), case studies (living
  design-validation fixtures in [`../docs/examples/case-studies/`](../docs/examples/case-studies/)),
  and ADRs (immutable records — a superseded ADR is amended in place, never moved).

## Contents

_Empty._ The July 2026 clean-sweep found **no file-level candidates**: every architecture doc and ADR
is current, research docs were kept in place under banners, case studies are living fixtures, and the
architecture BRIEF was retained as the canonical quick-reference (its ~200 inbound section references
made retirement more costly than keeping it). This directory and its convention exist so that a
future sweep has a designated home for genuinely retired files.
